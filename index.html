French Club at UIC

Who Are We?

We are a club at the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC) dedicated to French/Francophone culture. We have no membership costs or requirements. There is no knowledge of French language required.


We host two events a month during the semester. One of those events is always off-campus and the other is on-campus. On-campus events include, but are not limited to, film screenings, holiday parties, and LOTS OF FOOD! Off-campus events are typically hosted by a third party for a discounted price. These events have included French operas, concerts, restaurant visits, and book signings.
